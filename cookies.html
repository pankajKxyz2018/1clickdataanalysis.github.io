Cookie Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2025  |  Last Updated: March 10, 2025

Plain English Summary: We use minimal cookies — only what's needed to keep you logged in and understand how our product is used. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how people use the site.

2. Cookies We Use

Cookie NameTypePurposeDuration
sb-auth-token Essential Keeps you logged in to your account (set by Supabase authentication) 7 days
sb-refresh-token Essential Renews your login session securely without requiring re-authentication 30 days
_ga, _gid Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand page traffic and improve the product. No personal data is shared. 2 years / 24 hrs
cf_clearance Essential Set by Cloudflare to verify you're a human visitor and protect against bots 30 minutes
plan_pref Preference Remembers your selected plan during the checkout flow Session

3. Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • ❌ Advertising or retargeting cookies (Facebook Pixel, Google Ads, etc.)
  • ❌ Third-party social media tracking cookies
  • ❌ Cookies that build profiles about your browsing habits across websites
  • ❌ Any cookies that sell or share your data with advertisers

4. Your Data in the App (Streamlit)

When you use our analysis app at the app link, Streamlit may set its own session cookies to maintain your analysis session. These are:

  • Temporary (session-only)
  • Required for the app to function
  • Not used for tracking or advertising

Any data you upload is processed in-memory and is not stored after your session ends.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions
⚠️ Note: Blocking essential cookies (like auth tokens) will prevent you from logging in to your account. Analytics cookies can be blocked without affecting functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website and app integrate with the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics — Usage analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — Security and CDN (Privacy Policy)
  • Streamlit — App hosting (Privacy Policy)

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add new features or integrations. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our website.
