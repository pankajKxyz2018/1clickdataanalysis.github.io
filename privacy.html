Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Effective: March 2025  ·  1 Click Data Analysis

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

1 Click Data Analysis is an AI-powered data analytics platform operated by Pankaj Kumar Das, based in India. You can contact us at [email protected].

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, company name, email address and password (encrypted).

Uploaded Data: Files you upload (CSV, Excel, etc.) are processed to generate analysis. We do not read, share or use this data for any purpose other than running your analysis.

Usage Data: We collect anonymised usage metrics such as features used, number of uploads, and session duration to improve the product.

Communication: If you contact us by email, we retain that correspondence to respond to you.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the 1 Click Data Analysis platform
  • To authenticate your login and manage your account
  • To process your uploaded data and return analysis results
  • To send important account and service notifications
  • To improve the platform based on anonymised usage patterns
  • To respond to your support requests and enquiries

We do not use your data to train AI models. We do not sell or rent your data to any third party.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard encryption. Your uploaded files are processed in memory and are not permanently stored on our servers beyond your session.

We implement the following security measures:

  • Multi-tenant architecture — each company's data is isolated
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access controls ensure only you can see your data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your account data is deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymised usage statistics may be retained for analytics
  • You can request a copy of your data before deletion

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database (GDPR compliant)
  • Streamlit Cloud — App hosting (Snowflake Inc.)
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting (Microsoft)
  • Cloudflare — DNS and security (no data processing)
  • Google Fonts — Typography (no personal data shared)

We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels on our platform.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect login functionality.
