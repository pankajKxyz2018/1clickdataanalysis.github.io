Our Workplace Policy

How we work, where we work, and what we stand for — as a team.

✅ Effective: January 2024  ·  1 Click Data Analysis  ·  Version 1.0
🌍
100% Remote Work
Work from anywhere in India

1 Click Data Analysis is a fully remote company. There is no fixed office. Every team member — current and future — works from their preferred location, whether that's home, a co-working space, or anywhere else they choose.

  • No mandatory office attendance — ever
  • All collaboration happens asynchronously through digital tools
  • Team members across any city or state in India are welcome
  • Equipment and connectivity are the responsibility of the individual
⏰
Flexible Working Hours
Results over clock-watching

We don't track hours. We track outcomes. Team members are free to structure their day as they see fit, as long as they meet their commitments and communicate proactively.

  • No fixed start or end time
  • Core overlap window: 11am–4pm IST for real-time collaboration when needed
  • Deadlines and deliverables take priority over schedules
  • Personal commitments and time zones are respected
💬
Async-First Communication
Fewer meetings, more focus

We believe deep work is the source of our best output. Meetings are the last resort, not the first. Written communication — clear, direct, and documented — is how we operate.

  • Default to written updates and documented decisions
  • No unnecessary meetings — every call has a clear agenda
  • Response time expectation: within 4 business hours
  • All important decisions are documented in writing
🚀
Full Ownership & Autonomy
Move fast, take responsibility

We are a lean, fast-moving startup. Every person owns their work end-to-end — from planning to execution to quality. We hire for self-direction and trust people to deliver without micromanagement.

  • Every team member is the owner of their domain
  • Proactive communication is expected — no waiting to be asked
  • Mistakes are learning opportunities — honesty is valued over perfection
  • Initiative is celebrated; asking for permission is not the culture
🤝
Equal Opportunity & Inclusion
Merit above everything else

1 Click Data Analysis is an equal opportunity organisation. We do not discriminate on the basis of gender, religion, caste, ethnicity, disability, age, or any other personal characteristic. All hiring, compensation, and growth decisions are based solely on skills, work quality, and contribution.

  • Zero tolerance for discrimination or harassment of any kind
  • All voices are heard — seniority doesn't silence input
  • Pay is based on role and contribution, not background
  • Inclusive and respectful communication is non-negotiable
