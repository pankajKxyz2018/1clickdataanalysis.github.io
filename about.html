✦ Our Story

We Believe Data Is For Everyone.

Not just for companies with data science teams, six-figure budgets, or engineering departments. For the HR manager in Kolkata. The sales head in Chennai. The retailer in Surat.

The Story Behind 1 Click

On 30th September 2024, I left my job. Not because I had a plan. Because I had a problem I couldn't stop thinking about.

I had spent 7+ years in NLP and AI Data Operations — watching businesses struggle with the same thing over and over. They had data. Mountains of it. Excel files piling up on shared drives. Reports that took days to build. Insights that arrived too late to matter.

The tools that could help them — Power BI, Tableau, Python, R — required months of training, expensive licenses, or a dedicated data team. Small and mid-size businesses simply couldn't access them.

So I built 1 Click Data Analysis — solo, using AI-assisted development. A tool where anyone, regardless of technical background, can upload their business data and get boardroom-ready insights in 60 seconds. No coding. No training. No data science degree required. Just upload and go.

// In His Own Words

I am not a traditional software developer. I am a domain expert who learned to build software using AI — what the tech world now calls a Vibe Coder. My 7+ years in NLP and AI Data Operations gave me deep understanding of what businesses actually need from their data.

I built 1 Click Data Analysis entirely using AI-assisted development — Claude AI, Python, Streamlit, and Supabase. Every feature was designed from the perspective of a business user, not an engineer. Because I've sat in those meetings where the data analyst says "it'll be ready next week" and the decision can't wait.

This product is my answer to that problem. Built solo. Launched from Kolkata. Priced for Indian businesses. But designed to work for any business, anywhere in the world.
